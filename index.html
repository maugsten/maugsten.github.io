Welcome

As of 2024, I have started my PhD in Fluid Mechanics at the University of Oslo. My research focuses on Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS) technology, specifically studying how CO2 is transported in steeply inclined pipes. This project involves hands-on experimentation, data processing, and result analysis, bridging both practical applications and theoretical frameworks in fluid mechanics, while also exploring the promising prospects of CCS technology.
