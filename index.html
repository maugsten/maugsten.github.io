Welcome

As of 2024, I have started my PhD in Fluid Mechanics at the University of Oslo. My research focuses on CCS technology, specifically studying how CO2 is transported in steeply inclined pipes. This new project involves hands-on experimentation, data processing, and result analysis. It offers an exploration of both practical applications and theoretical frameworks in fluid mechanics and the promising prospects of CCS technology.
